# BrAINWARS-2026-Business-Strategy-Case
Business strategy case solution for BrAINWARS 2026, featuring market entry analysis, GTM strategy, AI positioning, and financial evaluation.

## Overview
Developed a data-driven business strategy for **PulseX Technologies**, an AI-powered fitness wearables company, as part of BrAINWARS 2026. The case involved identifying the optimal growth strategy, recommending a go-to-market (GTM) model, and positioning PulseAI to maximize long-term profitability and competitive advantage.

## Problem Statement
PulseX sought to determine the most viable growth path by evaluating:
- International market expansion
- Domestic ecosystem expansion
- Enterprise AI applications

The solution required balancing market opportunity, financial feasibility, AI scalability, and competitive positioning.

## Approach
- Conducted market attractiveness analysis across multiple regions.
- Compared markets using CAGR, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), demand projections, pricing power, and regulatory readiness.
- Built a weighted market scorecard to prioritize expansion opportunities.
- Evaluated multiple GTM models (D2C, Retail, Gym Partnerships, Hybrid).
- Recommended an AI positioning strategy supported by competitive benchmarking.

## Key Recommendation
- Recommended **India** as the primary launch market followed by **Europe**.
- Proposed a **Hybrid GTM model** combining D2C, Gym Partnerships, and Selective Retail.
- Positioned **PulseAI** as a performance differentiator to maximize subscription adoption and create a defensible data moat.
